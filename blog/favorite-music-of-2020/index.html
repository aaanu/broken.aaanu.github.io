Favorite music of 2020
Oct 11, 2020
Anu Gulati
4 minute read

< Updated on 11/27/2020 >

I’m aware there are two months left in 2020. Figured I’d document what music I’ve loved the most this year. I thought it was a pretty good music year, overwhelming with releases at times. I spent a lot of time not keeping up with 2020 music and being invested in 2000s rap instead, or Big L essentials, or Chilean trap. I also got really into Milwaukee rap while the rest of the country was catching up on Detroit rap thru TikTok.
